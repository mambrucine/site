LA FLIA

SHORT FILM
COMEDY DRAMA
ARGENTINA
2023
15'

Carlos, Paula, and their two kids, Juana and Fausto, get together around the table for dinner. The idea of a nice family dinner to celebrate the parents' anniversary collapses as the tensions arise along the night to reveal the cracks of a fragmented family.

ABOUT THE DIRECTOR

Tiago Suliansky is a director, scriptwriter, art director and costume designer, currently completing a degree in Film Direction in Universidad del Cine (Argentina). He’s the writer and director of two short films: La Flia (2023) -selected in international festivals and winner of the award to Best Student Short Film in the New York Tri-State Film Festival- and Jajan’t, that is today in postproduction. As Art Director he participated in short films as Quiero un pucho (2023) by Antonella Jiménez, Curitas (2023) and Tiempo (2023) by Juana Palazzi, Baila tu (2023) by Ezequiel Dykerman, Frontispicio (2024) by Juan Manuel Corvalan, Café con leche y dos medialunas (2024) by Camila Medrano and Ruido estatico (2025) by Mateo Garimbeti. He worked as Costume Designer for short films like Showroom (2023) by Caterina Diaz, Cráter (2023) by Valentina Cabado and Linaje (2023) by Camila Nuñez Montalbetti; Also in two feature films Memotest (2024) by Catalina Cortinez and Lo que nos queda (2023) by Violeta Cárcova & Nicolas Alan Medina and in the music video Sali con mi ex by musical artist CAE.

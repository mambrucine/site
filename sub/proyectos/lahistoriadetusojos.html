LA HISTORIA DE TUS OJOS

SHORT FILM
FICTION DRAMA
SPAIN
IN POSTPRODUCTION

The stars dance to the rhythm of the soundtrack of two girls who get lost and decide to camp. Romanticizing the fear that comes with the fleeting nature of the moment is always easier when we cling to the memory of the person beside us. Who decides when a story ends — your gaze or mine?

ABOUT THE DIRECTOR

Laura Oliver Urbano is a Valencian screenwriter and director focused on telling stories that make an impact in the world. She combines political science — a degree she studied across Spain, France, and the United States — with screenwriting. Her multidisciplinary approach is key to turning ideas into realities and bringing social change to the screen, telling stories that have yet to receive the space they deserve. A winner of several literary awards, including the “Vila de Mislata” Short Literature Prize (2021), the Mostra’t Gandia Festival (2019), and the Parkinson Leon Literary Contest (2018), among others, she is now aiming to turn her concerns into cinematic stories.

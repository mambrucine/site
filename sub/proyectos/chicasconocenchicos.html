SHORT FILM
FICTION COMEDY
ARGENTINA
IN POSTPRODUCTION

María, a young musical theatre student, is convinced she’s losing her memory. As she delves deeper into the rehearsals for a new play, her concern grows—along with an unexpected attraction to Mariángeles, the lead actress. Together, they embark on a quest to find a magical solution to María’s problem.

ABOUT THE DIRECTOR

Catalina Cortínez Tripodi (2001) is an Argentine director and screenwriter. A graduate of the Film Directing program at the Universidad del Cine, she received specialized training under the mentorship of Ingrid Pokropek and Malena Solarz. She has worked as a producer, assistant director, and script supervisor on various audiovisual projects. She directed three short films, including Majo y Alfonso (2024), which was part of the official selection at the 21st TAFIC. She is currently in post-production on her debut feature film, Memotest.

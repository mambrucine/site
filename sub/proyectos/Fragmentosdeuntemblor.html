FRAGMENTOS DE UN TEMBLOR (EX SOLAS)

SHORT FILM
FICTION-FAMILY DRAMA
ARGENTINA
IN POSTPRODUCTION

Three women, bound by blood and separated by their ages, face the aftermath of their matriarch’s suicide. Grief inhabits the silence between them, where memory trembles and gestures replace words. A fragmented portrait of intergenerational loss and the fragile hope of connection.

ABOUT THE DIRECTOR

Marbella Tescari (Caracas, 1994) is a film director and screenwriter based in Buenos Aires. She studies Filmmaking at the Universidad del Cine (FUC), where she also works as a teaching assistant in Film Aesthetics. Her short films Longing and Family Legacy were selected and produced by the university. Her third short film, Fragments of the aftershock, was selected for the ABC BAFICI post-production mentorships and participated in BAFICI’s distribution workshops. Her work explores family ties, grief, desire, and the silences that inhabit the body.
